[h2]VFE Stalker Edition Out now![/h2]
Ported version of Stalker included in the back.

With approval from Vilespring, and after months of work, I have ported VFE, one of the greatest addons in the community, to Build 42 (Unofficially).

This version serves as a temporary port while Vilespring works on officially updating VFE for Build 42, taking full advantage of all the new systems with skills far beyond mine.

Consider this a tribute to the great VFE and its Devs while official development continues.

[h2]About This Port[/h2]

I have taken the time to faithfully port all the core functionalities from the original Build 41 version, while also incorporating some of the new systems introduced in Build 42.

[b]Changes[/b]
[b]VFE[/b]
[list]
[*] JungleMags (Couple Mags) can be equipt with slings, due to new attachment categories introduce in B42.
[*] Ammo Cartons are introduced along with Packs, Cases and Crates as a cheaper alternative.
[/list]

[b]VFES[/b]
[list]
[*] New Caliber types for Rifles and the VSS, 7.62x54mm and 9x39mm can be found along the already existing 5.45mm
[*] Introduce same ammo packing logic to all new VFES calibers. they can now be condense into packs, cases, crates or cartons
[/list]

[b]VFE94[/b]
[list]
[*] Added Suppressors as an item that can be found. allowing you to alternate between the SD and NON SD variant of certain guns.
[*] Allowed ammo packing of new ammunitions as well.
[/list]

[h2]Additional Integration[/h2]

Both RAF and Hot Brass are marked as dependencies [b]BUT THEY ARE NOT[/b]. they are both optional to enhance the experience in the game.
